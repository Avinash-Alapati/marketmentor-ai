# Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)

## Definition

MACD is a momentum and trend-following indicator that helps traders identify changes in trend direction and momentum.

---

## Components

- MACD Line
- Signal Line
- Histogram

---

## Default Settings

- Fast EMA = 12
- Slow EMA = 26
- Signal EMA = 9

---

## Interpretation

Bullish Signal

MACD crosses above Signal Line.

Bearish Signal

MACD crosses below Signal Line.

---

## Histogram

Positive Histogram

Bullish momentum increasing.

Negative Histogram

Bearish momentum increasing.

---

## Advantages

- Combines trend and momentum
- Easy to identify crossovers
- Widely used

---

## Limitations

- Lagging indicator
- False signals in sideways markets

---

## Beginner Mistakes

- Ignoring trend direction
- Trading every crossover

---

## Related Topics

- EMA
- RSI

---

## Key Takeaway

MACD works best when combined with market structure and higher timeframe trend analysis.
